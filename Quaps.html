Que the Music

It was a dismal day in Quishi, windy, drizzly, and foul smelling.

The field of Quaps stinky flowers had all just blossomed in unison as if instructed by a mean chef

with no nose, or one so small as to be invisible, or so nonfunctional as to be useless on a good day.

The low flying Kreps were unprepared for the body stunning malodor,

viciously and suddenly released into the air and they fell like rain plops

as soon as they passed through the stinky atmosphere, landing on the ground,

momentarily stunned, but quickly self revived as if swimming in smelling salts.

They stood up as quickly as they recovered and pinched their noses to seal out the smell.

Crying and screaming from pinching so hard,

alternating blowing their noses...honKKKKK,,,honKKKKK, honKKKK...

and looking frantically which direction was the shortest route out of the Quap patch.

Their eyes squinting in tears as if sprayed by a minced onion perfume,

they tried to coordinate with each other which way to go.

This was very difficult to do as the honKKKKings got louder and louder,

as more Kreps helplessly fell from the sky.

As the shifting winds corralled the stench always over the Kreps,

there was no good way out of this predicament.

They had to rely on the assistance and ingenuity of those remaining

but slower still floating and flapping Kreps that had not yet been affected by this fetor.

Oh to have no nose....it would have been better....but such was not the case....

for every living Krep had at least one nose, and always less than 2.

It would have been a complete disaster if not for the littlest Krep,

who had not yet been afflicted by the Quap's air borne attack.

Too little to keep up with the flitter, but also aware of the impending fate

that would also affect him/her if he/she did not change course and

avoid the aerial calamity and chorus of honKKKKing Kreps, now in complete disarray.

She/he was slow in speed, but quick in planning....

She/he always was, and now was no different,

as a plan started to form in her/his head.

( Dear reader...Kreps when they are little are always a combination of girl and boy,

which makes them much smarter as child Kreps than if they were either a girl or a boy.

Later on in life they will decide which to be,

depending on what is more fun or interesting for them,

but until they are adults....they are MUCH, MUCH smarter

than the adults with at least 2 points of view for every problem

and at least 10 solutions, depending on the day, what they ate for lunch,

if they were tired or there were fairies or dragons watching.

(More of that later.)
